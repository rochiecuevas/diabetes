Obesity in children between 2 and 4 years old was highest in Alaska in 2012. There, roughly 21 out of 100 toddlers exhibited BMI at or above the 95th percentile for their age group. On the other hand, Utah reported the least number of children of the same age group classified as obese (8.7%). Only two states reported childhood obesity below 10%: Utah and Colorado.
